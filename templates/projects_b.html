Projects across Sub-Saharan Africa

Join the Pipe Foundation provides Bluepump installations across Sub-Saharan Africa to millions of people. After more than ten years, Bluepumps have been installed within different projects across different rural communities.

Designed for long-lasting and reliable operation, Bluepumps can be encountered in over a dozen Sub-Saharan African countries such as Kenya and Tanzania.
