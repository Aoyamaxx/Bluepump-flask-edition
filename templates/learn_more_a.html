Fund Information

When individuals decide to donate to the Join the Pipe Foundation, it empowers the Foundation to continue with its mission and objectives; to provide individuals in less fortunate situations with the ability to have access to clean drinking water.

Join the Pipe Foundation tries to provide Bluepumps to as many rural communities as possible. With Africa’s Sub-Saharan climate, some Bluepumps are installed within the harshest environments in the world.

Every donation makes an impact, no matter the amount. With each donation, the Foundation can use these proceeds to maintain Bluepumps in Sub-Saharan Africa or install additional Bluepumps for individuals that need them the most.

Join the Pipe Foundation works with its partners within Sub-Saharan Africa to assess the condition of the Bluepumps currently installed. If Bluepumps are non-operational, the Foundation coordinates with its partners to repair the Bluepump as quickly as possible. If communities require a Bluepump, the Foundation coordinates with its partners and local community members to assess the most optimal location for the Bluepump.

As you can imagine, each assessment and coordination effort costs money. Each location and climate requires a different amount of materials for installing Bluepumps. Each area and community uses the Bluepump frequently in different intervals, which requires more effort to understand the situation of each Bluepump.

The Bluepump was first developed in 2003. Twenty years later, the Bluepump is still available in Sub-Saharan Africa, and we would like to continue to provide this handpump.
